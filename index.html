What is It?

Sustainable Developement Goal 8 (SDG 8), or "Decent Work and Economic Growth," aims to promote sustained, inclusive, and sustainable economic growth, full and productive employment, and decent work for all. The goal includes eradicating forced labor, modern slavery, and human trafficking and ending child labor. To achieve SDG 8, countries and stakeholders need to focus on promoting policies and investments that support job creation, entrepreneurship, innovation, and resource efficiency while addressing inequalities and ensuring decent working conditions..
